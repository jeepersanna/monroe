Monroe Elementary · Community Hub

We take care
of each other.

Need something? We've got you.
Want to help? We'll find a match.
Free. Confidential. In English, Spanish, and Cantonese.

The Community Hub brings together community care, resources, and volunteer opportunities for Monroe families — all in one place, free, and available in English, Spanish, and Chinese.

Use the tabs below to find support and services, connect with the equity fund, browse curated resources, volunteer, or check the school lunch menu.
