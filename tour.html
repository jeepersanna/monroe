What to expect on your visit

⏱
Duration

Tours run about 45 minutes and include a walk through classrooms, the yard, the library, and the garden.

👨‍👩‍👧
Who should come

You're welcome to bring your child. We love meeting prospective students. Younger siblings are welcome too.

🌐
Languages

Tours available in English, Spanish, and Cantonese. Let us know your preference when you sign up.

📋
Next steps

After your tour, we'll share enrollment information and connect you with our family liaisons.

Schedule a Visit

Fill out the form and we'll reach out within 2 business days to confirm a time.

Preferred Visit Dates

Tours are held Tuesday–Thursday mornings during the school year. We'll do our best to match your preferred date.
