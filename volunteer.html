Monroe Elementary · Volunteer

Show up for Monroe.

Whether you have an hour or a whole morning, there's a place for you here. Fill out the form below and we'll match you with opportunities that fit your schedule and interests.

Ways to get involved

Find what fits your time, energy, and talents.

🎨
In the Classroom

Help teachers during reading groups, art projects, or classroom activities. Work one-on-one with students or support small group learning.

Background check required
🎉
Events & Fundraisers

Set up, run booths, or help clean up at Book Fair, Harvest Festival, Field Day, and more. Every event runs on community energy.

📚
Library

Shelve books, help during library time, or assist with the school book room. A quiet, meaningful way to support a love of reading.

🏃
Yard Duty & Field Trips

Supervise recess, walk with classes, or chaperone off-campus adventures. Your presence keeps kids safe and makes new experiences possible.
