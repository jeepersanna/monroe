Three easy ways to give

How to contribute

1

Check or Cash

Place cash or a check (payable to Monroe Elementary PTA) in the red envelope and return it in your child's orange folder.

Return by January 9th — no envelope? Just seal it in any envelope with your child's name and teacher on the front.

2

Text to Give

Text the code below to 33100 — it takes about 30 seconds.

Text: monroe25

You'll receive a link to complete your donation securely from your phone.

3

Give Online

The fastest way — takes just a minute with a credit or debit card.

Donate at RallyUp →

Or scan the QR code posted at school and on the bulletin board outside the office.

💼
Double Your Impact with an Employer Match

Many employers — including Google, Salesforce, Apple, and hundreds more — will match your donation dollar for dollar to a qualifying school or nonprofit. That means your $50 gift could become $100 for Monroe students.

Check with your HR department or employee portal to see if your company participates. Monroe Elementary PTA is a registered 501(c)(3) — our EIN is available on request by emailing the PTA.
