Your guide to ParentSquare

ParentSquare is how Monroe's school and PTA reaches families. Not sure how to join? We have a step-by-step tutorial. Already in but finding it confusing to navigate? We cover that too.

What is ParentSquare?

ParentSquare is Monroe's official communication platform — used by the school office, teachers, and the PTA. When something important is happening, you'll hear about it here first. Messages arrive by text, email, or app notification in the language you choose.

ParentVue is a separate SFUSD system for viewing grades, attendance, and forms. We cover that at the bottom of this page.

📢
School Announcements
Messages from the principal, office, and teachers delivered to your phone.
🗓️
Event Reminders
Never miss a field trip deadline, Picture Day, or Fall Fest again.
🌍
Your Language
Messages auto-translate to Spanish, Cantonese, Mandarin, and more.

Reply to teachers and staff, or message other parents directly.

How to join — step by step

Follow these steps on your phone or computer. It takes about 3 minutes.

1

Download the app

Search for ParentSquare in the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). It's free. Tap Install.

App Store ↗ Google Play ↗
2

Open the app and tap "Sign Up"

When you open the app, tap Sign Up at the bottom of the screen. Don't tap Log In — you don't have an account yet.

3

Enter your email or phone number

Use the same email address or phone number you gave to Monroe's school office when you enrolled. This is important — if the school doesn't have this info, the system won't recognize you. (See troubleshooting below if this happens.)

⚠️ Use the email/phone on file with the school — not a new one.
4

Check for a confirmation message

ParentSquare will send a 6-digit code to your email or phone. Open your messages, find the code, and type it into the app.

💡 Tip: Check your spam or junk folder if you don't see the email within 2 minutes.
5

Create a password

Choose a password and confirm it. Make it something you'll remember — you'll use it to log back in.

6

Set your language and notifications

Choose your preferred language for messages. Then decide how you want to receive notifications: by text, email, or app push notification. You can choose all three.

7

You're in! ✓

You should now see Monroe Elementary in your feed. You're connected and will receive all school and PTA messages from here on.

1

Go to the ParentSquare website

Open a browser and go to: parentsquare.com/join_school/new — or click the button below.

Go to ParentSquare.com ↗
2

Click "Sign Up"

On the homepage, click the blue Sign Up button in the top right corner.

3

Search for Monroe Elementary

In the school search field, type Monroe Elementary and select Monroe Elementary School – San Francisco, CA.

4

Enter your email

Enter the email address that's on file with the school. This must match what Monroe has in their records.

⚠️ Use the email on file with the school office.
5

Check your email for a confirmation code

You'll receive an email from ParentSquare with a verification code. Enter it on the website.

💡 Check spam/junk if it doesn't arrive within 2 minutes.
6

Create your account

Fill in your name, create a password, and choose your notification preferences and language.

7

Done! ✓

You'll be taken to your Monroe feed. You're now connected to all school and PTA communication.

Once you're in

How to use ParentSquare

Already have an account? Here's how to find messages, reach your child's teacher, RSVP to events, and manage your settings.

📬
Finding messages and announcements

When you open the app, you land on your Home feed. This shows all recent posts from Monroe — school announcements, PTA updates, event info, and classroom news, all in one place, newest first.

Tap any post to open it and read the full message. Attachments (flyers, forms, sign-ups) appear as buttons at the bottom of the post.

The bell icon (top right corner) shows your unread notifications. A red dot means something new is waiting.

💬
Messaging your child's teacher

Tap the speech bubble icon at the bottom of the screen (it may say "Messages" or "Direct Messages").

  1. Tap the pencil / compose icon (top right)
  2. Type your teacher's name in the search box
  3. Select them from the list and write your message
  4. Tap Send — they'll receive it in their ParentSquare account
💡 Teachers typically respond within 1–2 school days. For urgent matters, call the school office directly.
📅
RSVPing to events

When the school or PTA posts an event that needs a response, you'll see a RSVP button (or "Yes / No / Maybe") inside the post.

Tap your response — it's recorded automatically. Some events also let you sign up to volunteer or bring items (like food for a party). Those show a separate Sign Up button.

📎
Opening forms and attachments

Many school posts include forms, permission slips, or sign-up sheets. They appear as orange or blue buttons inside the post (e.g. "Fill Out Form" or "View Document").

Tap the button — the form opens inside the app or in your browser. Fill it out there and tap Submit. You don't need to print anything.

⚠️ Some forms have deadlines. Check the post for the due date before closing it.
🌍
Changing your language

ParentSquare can auto-translate all messages into your language. To set it:

  1. Tap your profile photo (top right corner)
  2. Tap Settings
  3. Tap Language
  4. Choose your preferred language from the list

Messages will now appear in your language. You can switch back to English any time.

🔔
Managing notifications

Getting too many pings? Or not getting any? Here's how to adjust:

  1. Tap your profile photo → Settings → Notification Settings
  2. Choose whether to receive by text, email, app notification, or a combination
  3. You can also set Quiet Hours (e.g. no pings after 8pm)
  4. To mute a specific group or class, tap Schools & Groups and toggle off the ones you don't want
🔍
Finding an older message

Tap the magnifying glass / search icon at the top or bottom of the app. Type any keyword (like "field trip" or "Picture Day") and ParentSquare will show all matching past posts.

You can also scroll back through your Home feed — posts are stored indefinitely, so nothing disappears.

📬
Finding messages and announcements

Log in at parentsquare.com. Your Home feed is on the left side of the screen — all Monroe posts, newest first.

Click any post to expand it. Attachments, forms, and sign-up buttons appear inside the post.

The notification bell in the top menu bar shows unread items.

💬
Messaging your child's teacher

In the left sidebar, click Messages (or the speech bubble icon).

  1. Click the pencil icon to start a new message
  2. Type the teacher's name in the "To:" field
  3. Select them from the dropdown and type your message
  4. Click Send
💡 You can also reply to a school announcement by scrolling to the bottom of the post and using the comment/reply box.
📅
RSVPing to events

Open the event post in your feed. You'll see Yes / No / Maybe buttons (or an RSVP form). Click your answer — it saves automatically.

For volunteer or item sign-ups, click the Sign Up button inside the post and follow the prompts. You'll receive a confirmation and a reminder closer to the date.

📎
Opening forms and attachments

Click the post containing the form. Look for a colored button labeled "Fill Out Form," "View Attachment," or "Open Document" — it's usually near the bottom of the post content.

Forms open in a new tab. Fill them out and submit there — no printing required.

⚠️ Check the deadline in the post before closing. Submitted forms go directly to the school or teacher.
🌍
Changing your language

Click your profile photo or initials (top right corner) → Account Settings → Language.

Select your language from the dropdown and save. All future posts will auto-translate. You can switch languages at any time.

🔔
Managing notifications

Click your profile photo → Account Settings → Notifications.

  • Toggle on/off Email, Text, and Push notifications separately
  • Set Quiet Hours so you aren't pinged in the evenings
  • To stop notifications from a specific group, go to Schools & Groups and click the toggle next to that group
🔍
Finding an older message

Use the search bar at the top of the page. Type a keyword (e.g., "field trip," "Fall Fest," "lunch") and ParentSquare will show all matching posts.

You can also browse the Calendar section (left sidebar) to see upcoming and past events.

Having trouble? Here's what to try.

"ParentSquare says it can't find my email or phone."

This means the contact info you entered doesn't match what Monroe has on file. Here's what to do:

  1. Check if you used a different email when you enrolled (work email, spouse's email, etc.)
  2. Try your phone number instead of email, or vice versa
  3. Contact the Monroe office at (415) 469-4736 or sch729@sfusd.edu to confirm what's on file — they can update it for you
  4. Ask one of our Family Liaisons for help — they do this regularly
"I never received the confirmation code."
  • Check your spam or junk mail folder
  • Make sure you're checking the right inbox (some families have multiple email accounts)
  • Wait 5 minutes and try again — sometimes there's a delay
  • Try requesting the code again from the app or website
  • If using phone/text: make sure the number is correct and can receive texts
"I already have a ParentSquare account from another school."

Great! You just need to add Monroe. Log in to your existing account, go to Settings → Schools, and search for Monroe Elementary. Your existing account works across multiple schools.

"The app won't download / my phone is old."

ParentSquare also works in a web browser — go to parentsquare.com on any device. You don't need the app.

"I set it up but I'm not getting any messages."
  • Check your notification settings in the app (tap your profile photo → Notification Settings)
  • Make sure notifications are enabled for ParentSquare in your phone's Settings app
  • Check that you're enrolled in Monroe Elementary specifically
  • Contact us and we'll help troubleshoot
"I need help in Spanish / 我需要中文帮助"

Our Family Liaisons can help you set up ParentSquare in person, in your language. Contact them through the school office at (415) 469-4736 — ask for a family liaison.

ParentVue — a different system

ParentVue is separate from ParentSquare. It's run by SFUSD (the district) and gives you access to your child's records.

  • ✓ Update emergency contact information
  • ✓ View attendance records
  • ✓ Access report cards
  • ✓ Find your child's student ID and Google password
  • ✓ Complete online forms and applications
  • ✓ Receive district-level communications

How to get access:

You need a unique access code from the school. Contact Monroe's office or a Family Liaison to get yours.
