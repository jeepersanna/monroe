Monroe Jog-a-Thon

The PTA’s biggest fall fundraiser — an all-school morning where every kid runs laps for pledges. Costumes encouraged. Last year we raised $18K and ran a collective 4,200 laps. Goal this year: 5,000.

A year of Monroe events.

A sampling of events Monroe hosts throughout the school year — to support student achievement, recognize student talents, and celebrate our diverse community.

Fall

Back to School Night

First chance to meet your child’s teacher and walk the classroom.

Fall

Fall Fun Festival

Schoolyard carnival with games, food trucks, and the PTA pumpkin patch.

Year-round

Walk to School Days

Monthly walking-school-bus mornings, organized by neighborhood.

Year-round

Community playdates & garden workdays

Weekend mornings in the yard and garden — all families welcome.

Winter / Spring

Winter & spring performances

Music and dance showcases by grade, in the auditorium.

Year-round

Movie nights

Cafeteria popcorn, blankets on the floor, family-friendly picks.

Winter

Lunar New Year celebration

Lion dance, dumplings, and red envelopes — led by our Chinese pathway families.

Winter

Black Scholars celebration

An annual recognition of Black student achievement at Monroe.

Fall / Spring

Heritage celebrations

Hispanic Heritage and Native American Heritage events — performances, food, art.

Spring

Read-a-thon

A schoolwide reading challenge — library books, pledge sheets, classroom totals.

Fall

Annual Fund

The PTA’s once-a-year ask — funds classroom enrichment, scholarships, and the garden.

May

K & 5th grade promotion

The end-of-year ceremonies for our youngest and oldest classes.
