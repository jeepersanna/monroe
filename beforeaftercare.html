Before & after school programs.

Monroe doesn't run extended-day programming itself — we partner with longstanding local organizations to do it well. Two programs run right on campus; three others pick up from Monroe at the last bell.

Every program participates in SFUSD's subsidy system. Most are free for families who qualify for free or reduced-price lunch. If cost is a concern, we'll walk you through it.

Getting started

How to enroll, how to pay less, and who to call.

Registration

YMCA enrollment

  • Registration opens July 19th. Spots fill quickly — sign up the day enrollment opens.
  • New families: create an account at community.ymcasf.org.
  • Returning families: log in with last year's credentials and confirm your child's info.
  • More details at ymcasf.org/afterschool.
Financial Aid

Most families pay little or nothing

  • Verify income at SchoolCafe.com — this unlocks free/reduced lunch and subsidized after-care at once.
  • The YMCA offers sliding-scale tuition. Submit the aid application alongside enrollment.
  • OST, Boys & Girls Club, and Rec Connect all honor SFUSD income verification.
  • Our family liaisons can help with paperwork in English, Spanish, or Cantonese.
