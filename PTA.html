Our School & Programs / PTA

The Monroe PTA.

A volunteer-run 501(c)(3) of Monroe parents, caregivers, and teachers — funding the things the district can't, and building community while we're at it.

We fund the art supplies, the field trip buses, the garden, the noon-yard equipment, and the after-school enrichment scholarships. We run the Annual Fund, the Book Fair, the Jog-a-Thon, and Family Pupusa Night. All parents and caregivers are members the moment they walk in.

Free to join — optional $25 dues for voting membership
Monthly meetings — last Wednesday, 6pm, open to all
$42K annual goal — funds 4 program areas
10+ volunteer events — year-round, flexible commitments

What the PTA funds.

Four pillars

Everything we raise goes into one of these four buckets — voted on each year by the board, in consultation with the principal.

i.

Classroom enrichment

Art supplies beyond the district allotment, science kits, classroom libraries, field trip buses, and the music program. The line item that makes Monroe feel like Monroe.

ii.

School life

The Friday assembly sound system, schoolyard equipment, beautification projects, and the Welcome Picnic in August. The connective tissue.

iii.

Equity & scholarships

After-school care scholarships, field trip waivers, school-supply drives, and translation services for our family meetings.

iv.

Garden & outdoors

Garden teacher hours, beds and soil, the chicken coop’s upkeep, and our partnership with the Excelsior Garden Network.

Three ways to get involved.

Whether you have ten dollars or ten hours, there’s a way to contribute. All three matter.

01. Join

Become a member.

Every Monroe parent or caregiver is automatically eligible. The optional $25 dues registration adds your name to the voting rolls and the directory — it also covers your seat at the annual gala.

Membership gets you the monthly newsletter, voting rights at meetings, and a place in the family directory (opt-in).

$25 / family
Or pay-what-you-can

02. Volunteer

Sign up for a shift.

Book Fair check-in, Jog-a-Thon water table, Pupusa Night prep, classroom reading buddy, or yard-beautification Saturdays — every event has a sign-up sheet on ParentSquare.

One-off helpers are just as welcome as committee chairs. Tell us when you’re free; we’ll match you to a role.

As little as 1 hr
Flexible · year-round

03. Donate

Give to the Annual Fund.

Our Annual Fund covers the four pillars above. The suggested gift is $250 per child per year — but gifts of every size add up, and every contribution is tax-deductible.

Many employers match. Ask your HR team about a corporate match before you give — it can double your impact at no cost to you.

$250 / child
Tax-deductible · suggested

Meetings

Monthly — open to all.

We meet the last Wednesday of every month at 6:00 p.m. in the Monroe library. Meetings run about 90 minutes. Childcare is provided in the cafeteria, snacks too.

Agendas go out on ParentSquare the Monday before. You don’t need to be a paid member to attend or to speak — only to vote.
